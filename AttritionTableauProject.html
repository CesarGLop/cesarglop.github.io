Employee Attrition Analysis Using Tableau

This project aims to analyze data through advanced algorithms and generate machine-learning models to help achieve better results. The goal is to create a system to reduce employee attrition.

Tools used:
 - MS Excel
 - Python
 - Machine Learning
 - Tableau

Observations:
 - Total Employee Attrition: 61 people.
 - Rate Employee Attrition: 16%.
 - Euros at risk by attrition: 598,685 ‚Ç¨

Key findings: Employee Attrition Profile:
 - The age range is 26 to 35 years old.
 - The marital status is Single.
 - The level of education is Universitario.
 - Live near-by from home.
 - Experience is 1 year.
 - Has been 1 year in the company.
 - Belongs to the Research and Development Department.
 - Laboratory Technician, Sales Executive, Research Scientist, and Sales Representative are job roles with more attrition scoring.
 - Work overtime.
 - The salary increase was around 10%.
 - Has had about 2 trainings per year.
 - Feels satisfied with his partners and work, but not with the environment.
 - Travels rarely per work.
