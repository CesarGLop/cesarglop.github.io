Data Professional Survey Breakdown using Power BI

This project aims to collect, clean, and analyze Data Professional survey data in order to find trends and patrons inside this community.

Tools used:
 - MS Excel
 - Data Manipulation
 - Data Cleaning
 - Power BI (DAX, Power Query, Visualization)

Observations:
 - Total respondents: 504 people.
 - Average age of interviewers: 30 years.
 - The role of Data Analyst represents 62% of those surveyed.
 - The largest numbers of ethnicities consulted were white or Caucasian and Asian or Asian American.
 - 61% of the people consulted made a career change to the Data Science Field.

Key findings: Employee Attrition Profile:
 - The highest paying jobs are data scientist and data engineer.
 - Python is the favorite programming language to use in the field of Data Science.
 - The country with the largest number of Data Scientists is the USA.
 - In this industry, there is no pay inequality, both women and men receive approximately the same salary.
 - Considering variables such as coworkers, learning new things, management, work-life balance, and salary, people feel comfortable, but
  not excited.
 - The main interest in making a job change is the salary.
 - The best salaries are in the US, Canada, and the UK in that order.
